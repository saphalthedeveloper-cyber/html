Learn Guitar

  1. step 1
    Learn these open chords: E, A, D, G, C, Em, Am.
    Practice switching between two chords slowly (e.g., Em ↔ G).
    Play simple down-strum on each chord (4 beats per chord).
  2. step 2
    Work on smooth chord changes using a metronome.
    Learn a couple of strumming patterns: steady downbeats, then down-up patterns.
    Start playing 2–3 simple songs that use only 2–4 chords.
  3. step 3
    Blend rhythm and lead: play rhythm while adding simple fills.
    Work on fingerstyle or Travis picking (if interested).
    Learn basic music theory: intervals, key, how chords fit in a key.
    Play with backing tracks / jam with others.
